# Technical lighting and light slots: when to use and how to specify

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Architecture & Design*

---

## Introduction

Technical lighting is not just an aesthetic effect. It organizes the reading of space, enhances materials, and creates precise sensations. The light slot is one of the most sophisticated solutions when well dimensioned.

---

## What is a light slot

It is a linear opening in the ceiling that houses recessed fixtures, creating a **continuous** and clean focus. The result is elegant, with diffuse light and no visual interference.

---

## When it makes sense to use

- Hallways and circulation areas with a clean language
- Rooms that need uniform and discreet light
- Spaces with straight-line millwork
- Minimalist and contemporary projects

---

## What must be well specified

- Slot width and depth
- Fixture type and color temperature
- Distance from walls and symmetry axes
- Interference with beams, ducts, and automation

---

## Conclusion

The light slot is a high-impact detail when executed with technical rigor. In premium projects, light is also architecture.

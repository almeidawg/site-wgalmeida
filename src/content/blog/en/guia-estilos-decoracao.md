# Complete Guide to Decor Styles: Find Yours

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Interior Design*

---

## Introduction

Style is more than appearance. It is how space translates routine, preferences, and sensoriality. In premium projects, style guides materials, proportions, and finish.

---

## Most sought-after styles

### Contemporary
Clean lines, balanced materials, and a current atmosphere without excess.

### Minimalist
Few elements, high quality, and visual silence.

### Cosmopolitan
Sophisticated mix of references, with personality and curation.

### Provencal
Romantic, delicate, and welcoming, with soft tones and texture.

### Industrial
Exposed structure, concrete, and metal, with urban language.

---

## How to choose your style

- Analyze your routine and real needs
- Gather references that repeat
- Prioritize materials you like to touch and see
- Avoid trends that do not match your way of living

---

## Conclusion

A well-defined style creates unity, elegance, and longevity. The project gains identity and becomes more coherent in every detail.

## 6. Classic Style

### Main characteristics
Timeless elegance, symmetry, and **traditional sophistication**.

**Key elements:**
- Rigorous symmetry
- Moldings and boiseries
- Noble fabrics (silk, velvet)
- Furniture with ornate details
- Crystal chandeliers

**Preferred materials:**
- Dark noble woods
- Marble
- Crystal
- Silk and velvet
- Gold and silver

**Ideal for those who:**
- Appreciate traditional elegance
- Value antique pieces
- Like formal environments
- Prefer discreet luxury
- Seek decor that lasts through generations

---

## How to Choose Your Style?

### Ask yourself:

1. **What is your routine like?**
   - Busy -> Minimalist or Contemporary
   - Calm -> Provencal or Classic

2. **What do you collect?**
   - Nothing -> Minimalist
   - Souvenirs -> Cosmopolitan
   - Antiques -> Classic or Provencal

3. **Which color attracts you?**
   - Neutrals -> Contemporary or Minimalist
   - Pastels -> Provencal
   - Vibrant -> Cosmopolitan

4. **Where do you feel good?**
   - Modern hotels -> Contemporary
   - Country houses -> Provencal
   - Urban lofts -> Industrial

---

## You Dont Have to Choose Just One!

The current trend is the **mix of styles**. You can have a contemporary base with industrial touches, or a provencal environment with minimalist elements.

The secret is to maintain **coherence** and **balance**.

# Construction schedule: how to track phases and deadlines with clarity

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

Premium works need predictability. A well-defined schedule organizes teams, purchases, and deliveries, avoiding stoppages and extra costs.

---

## What a good schedule must have

- Correct sequence of phases
- Realistic durations per phase
- Approval and delivery milestones
- Integration with purchasing and suppliers

---

## How to track efficiently

- Periodic updates with real status
- Visual and objective reports
- Deadline impact alerts when changes happen

---

## Why this protects the investment

A clear schedule reduces rework, avoids bottlenecks, and improves the final quality. It is a tool for control and transparency.

---

## Conclusion

Deadline management is part of operational excellence. When the schedule is well conducted, the work flows with predictability and elegance.

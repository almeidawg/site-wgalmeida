# Custom Millwork: When Personalization Makes All the Difference

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Premium Millwork*

---

## Introduction

Custom millwork is the point where architecture and materiality meet. It translates the project concept into **function, proportion, and finish**, ensuring visual unity and maximum space use.

---

## Custom, modular, or loose furniture

### Custom
- Exclusive design for your space
- Solutions for non-standard dimensions
- Full integration with the architecture

### Modular
- Modules with fixed dimensions
- Lower cost, less flexibility
- More limited finishes

### Loose furniture
- Ready delivery
- Little adaptation to the space
- Variable quality and durability

---

## Materials and finishes

### Structures
- **MDF**: ideal for paint and sophisticated finishes
- **MDP**: good cost-benefit for internal structures
- **Solid wood**: noble, durable, and with strong presence

### Premium finishes
- **Natural veneer**: texture and authenticity
- **Lacquer**: impeccable finish and custom colors
- **Glass**: visual lightness and refinement

---

## Solutions by environment

### Kitchen
Organized drawers, internal wire accessories, functional island, and integrated lighting.

### Closet
Smart dividers, overhead storage, sensor lighting, and built-in mirrors.

### Living and home office
Hidden cable routing, modular niches, and discreet sliding doors.

### Bathroom
Moisture-resistant materials, internal drawers, and hidden outlets.

---

## Hardware that elevates the result

- Soft-close hinges
- Heavy-duty telescopic slides
- Integrated handles or touch system

# Kitchen countertop: ergonomics, dimensions, and technical specs

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Architecture & Design*

---

## Introduction

In premium kitchens, the countertop sets the rhythm, ergonomics, and functionality. Correct dimensions reduce effort and increase efficiency in daily use.

---

## What must be well specified

- Height appropriate to the user
- Functional depth for prep work
- Correct distance between countertop and upper cabinets
- Materials resistant to heat and moisture

---

## Impact on everyday use

A well-planned countertop:

- Improves posture and comfort
- Organizes the prep flow
- Enhances the final finish

---

## Conclusion

Ergonomics and technical detail go together. In premium projects, the countertop is treated as a central piece of functionality.

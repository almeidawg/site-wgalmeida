# Executive Project: what it is, what it includes, and why it avoids rework

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

A good executive project is not the most complex. It is the most **executable**. It organizes the work, removes doubts, and turns intent into results.

---

## What an executive project is

It is the set of technical documents that guides the work with clarity. It defines **what will be done, how it will be done, and with which materials**, avoiding improvisation and extra costs.

> A quality executive project is clear, didactic, and oriented to the real sequence of the work.

---

## What typically composes the executive project

Content varies by scope, but usually includes:

- Layout plan and construction plan
- Floor and finish layouts
- Ceiling and lighting plan
- Electrical and plumbing installation map
- Room and millwork elevations
- Stonework, frames, and glass details
- Material and specification booklet

---

## Why it makes such a difference

- **Reduces rework** and execution errors
- **Protects the investment** with early definitions
- **Facilitates budgeting and purchasing**
- **Aligns team and client** in a single document

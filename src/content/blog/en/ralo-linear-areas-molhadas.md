# Linear drain and wet areas: correct specification avoids problems

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

The linear drain is a small detail with big impact. When poorly specified, it causes leaks, odors, and rework. When well planned, it improves finish and user experience.

---

## Essential technical points

- Drain sizing according to area
- Correct floor slope
- Well-executed waterproofing
- Access for maintenance and cleaning

---

## Where it makes the most sense

- Shower enclosures
- Gourmet areas
- Balconies and terraces
- Laundry rooms

---

## Conclusion

Drainage details protect the investment and elevate the final quality of the work. In premium projects, nothing is improvised.

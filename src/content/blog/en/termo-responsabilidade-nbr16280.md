# Responsibility term and NBR 16280: what the condominium requires

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

The responsibility term formalizes who is responsible for the renovation and ensures the work follows NBR 16280, the owner's manual, and the condominium's internal regulations.

---

## What normally appears in the term

- Declaration of responsibility for the impacts of the work
- Commitment to standards and current legislation
- Delivery of a renovation plan signed by a qualified professional
- ART/RRT and technical projects
- List of authorized professionals

---

## Why this protects the client

When the documentation is correct, the work moves forward with safety and predictability. This avoids interruptions and conflicts with the administration.

---

## Conclusion

The responsibility term is an essential part of construction governance. In premium projects, excellence includes compliance and documentary clarity.

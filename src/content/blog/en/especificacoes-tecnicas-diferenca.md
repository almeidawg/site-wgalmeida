# Technical specifications that make a difference in the final result

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Architecture & Design*

---

## Introduction

A premium finish is born in the specifications. They determine comfort, performance, and longevity of the project. When the detail is well defined, the work flows and the result remains flawless.

---

## Where specifications impact the most

### Lighting and ceiling
- Well-dimensioned light slots
- Proper color temperature
- Precise drops without unwanted shadows

### Wall and floor finishes
- Alignment of joints and cuts
- Clean material transitions without visual noise
- Correct choice of mortars and grout

### Millwork and hardware
- Correct thicknesses
- Technical clearances for operation
- High-performance hardware

### Stonework
- Thicknesses, chamfers, and finishes
- Sinks and drains well positioned
- Discreet, well-treated joints

---

## Why this protects the investment

Technical detailing reduces rework, avoids wrong purchases, and ensures consistency across all environments. It is what turns concept into construction without improvisation.

---

## Conclusion

In high-end projects, luxury is in precision. Clear specifications elevate quality and make the result durable and coherent.

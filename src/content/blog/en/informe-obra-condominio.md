# Condo work notice: how to fill it out and avoid delays

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

The work notice is the document that formalizes the renovation with the condominium. It reduces risks, standardizes information, and speeds approvals.

---

## Information normally required

- Type of intervention (demolition, electrical, plumbing, gas)
- Technical responsible (engineer or architect)
- Project delivery and ART/RRT
- Planned start and end date

---

## How to avoid delays

- Deliver complete documentation
- Keep the schedule realistic
- Update the condominium in case of changes

---

## Conclusion

A well-filled work notice ensures a smooth process and avoids administrative blocks. In premium works, organization starts at the documentation stage.

# Stages of a Full Renovation: From Start to Key Handover

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

A full renovation demands correct sequencing, technical planning, and quality control. When the phases are respected, the result gains predictability and a premium finish.

---

## Overview of stages

1. Planning  
2. Demolition and removals  
3. Infrastructure  
4. Partitions and subfloor  
5. Finishes  
6. Finalization  
7. Handover  

---

## 1. Planning

Phase in which the project is defined, materials are specified, and the schedule is validated. This is where rework is avoided.

---

## 2. Demolition and removals

Protection, authorized demolitions, disposal, and site preparation. Safety and control make all the difference.

---

## 3. Infrastructure

Electrical, plumbing, gas, air conditioning, and automation. Everything must be coordinated with the executive project.

---

## 4. Partitions and subfloor

Masonry or drywall, floor leveling, and waterproofing of critical areas.

---

## 5. Finishes

Finishes, ceilings, paint, millwork, stonework, and fixtures. Here detail defines the perception of quality.

---

## 6. Finalization

Final assemblies, lighting, fine cleaning, and delivery adjustments.

---

## 7. Handover

Full inspection, client manual, and final scope validation.

---

## Conclusion

Premium renovation is method. When each stage is respected, the work flows with quality and predictability.

## Stage 5: Finishes

**Typical duration:** 3-6 weeks

### Floor finishes

**Preparation:**
- Subfloor cleaning
- Primer application (if needed)
- Layout definition

**Installation:**
- Adhesive mortar suitable for the material
- Spacers for joints
- Cuts and trims
- Grouting

### Wall finishes

**Ceramic:**
- Surface preparation
- Installation
- Grouting
- Corner protection

**Paint:**
- Primer
- Joint compound (if needed)
- Sanding
- Paint coats

### Plaster and ceilings

**Types:**
- Conventional plaster ceiling
- Drywall ceiling
- Coving and tabica
- Light slot

**Sequence:**
- Support structure
- Plaster boards
- Joint finishing
- Painting

### Frames

**Doors:**
- Door frames and trims
- Door leaves
- Locks and handles
- Adjustment

**Windows:**
- Subframes (if not existing)
- Frame installation
- Sealing
- Glass

---

## Stage 6: Finalization

**Typical duration:** 2-3 weeks

### Sanitary ware and fixtures

**Bathrooms:**
- Toilets
- Basins (vessel or undermount)
- Faucets and mixers
- Showers
- Accessories (toilet paper holder, soap holder)

**Kitchen:**
- Sink and faucet
- Trap and valve

### Millwork

**Installation:**
- Module assembly
- Wall fixing
- Door and drawer adjustment
- Handle installation

**Check:**
- Door alignment
- Drawer operation
- Edge finishing
- Final cleaning

### Lighting

**Installation:**
- Chandeliers and pendants
- Spots and plafons
- Wall lights
- LED strips
- Automation (if any)

### Glass and mirrors

**Types:**
- Shower enclosures
- Vanity mirrors
- Decorative panels
- Glass doors

---

## Stage 7: Handover

**Typical duration:** 1 week

### Cleaning

**Rough cleaning:**
- Removal of residual debris
- Grout cleaning
- Removal of labels and protections

**Fine cleaning:**
- Glass cleaning
- Fixture cleaning
- Millwork cleaning
- Floor cleaning

### Inspection

**What to check:**
- Function of switches and outlets
- Function of faucets and flushes
- Door opening and closing
- Millwork alignment
- Paint finish
- Grout and trims

### Documentation

**Deliverables:**
- Owner's manual
- Material invoices
- Equipment warranties
- Technical assistance contacts
- Acceptance term

| **TOTAL** | **13 weeks** | **18-21 weeks** | **25 weeks** |

## Tips for a Smooth Renovation

### 1. Plan Well Before Starting
80% of construction problems come from insufficient planning.

### 2. Define Materials in Advance
Delivery delays are one of the biggest causes of stoppage.

### 3. Keep a Financial Buffer
Set aside 10-15% of the budget for contingencies.

### 4. Document Everything
Photos, emails, approvals in writing.

### 5. Visit the Site Regularly
Follow progress and resolve questions on the spot.

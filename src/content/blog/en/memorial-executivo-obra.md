# Executive memorial: objective, scope, and responsibilities of the work

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

The executive memorial is the document that consolidates the **scope of the work**, defining what will be executed, which resources will be used, and the responsibilities of each party.

---

## Objective of the executive memorial

Clearly and technically, the memorial must record:

- Services that will be executed
- Specialized labor and tools involved
- Materials needed for renovation or adaptation
- Limits of the approved scope

This ensures predictability, reduces risks, and protects the client's investment.

---

## Client contributions

For the work to move forward efficiently, the client needs to provide:

- Authorizations for team access
- Descriptive memorial of the property (when available)
- Drawings, presentation files, and existing documents
- Technical information about structure, electrical, and plumbing

---

## Why this document is decisive

A well-built memorial avoids different interpretations during the work. It aligns expectations, ensures transparency, and sustains the final quality of delivery.

---

## Conclusion

The executive memorial is an essential part of a premium process. It organizes scope, protects deadlines, and ensures each stage is executed with clarity.

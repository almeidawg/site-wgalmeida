# What Is Turn Key? The Complete Renovation System

*By Grupo WG Almeida | Engineering & Execution*

---

## Introduction

Turn Key is the model where a single company is responsible for **design, execution, and delivery**. You receive the space ready, with controlled quality, real timelines, and transparent budget. In practice, it is the most elegant way to conduct a complex work with predictability.

---

## How Turn Key works

### Traditional model (fragmented)

In the traditional model, the client coordinates several suppliers and becomes the manager of the work. This generates:

- Risk of incompatibilities between design and execution
- Fragmented and less predictable budgets
- Timelines subject to rework

### Turn Key model (integrated)

In Turn Key, **one company assumes full responsibility**:

1. Architectural and interior design
2. Technical and budget planning
3. Construction execution
4. Custom millwork
5. Installations and automation
6. Finalization and delivery

---

## Real benefits of Turn Key

- **Single responsible party** for final delivery
- **Fixed budget** with control of variables
- **Coherent schedule** with the scope
- **Standardized quality** in all stages
- **Less wear** and more time for the client

---

## Stages of the Turn Key project

### 1. Briefing and diagnosis
Meetings, technical visit, and scope definition.

### 2. Design
Concept, moodboard, detailing, and approval.

### 3. Work planning
Final budget, schedule, and purchasing.

### 4. Execution
Demolitions, infrastructure, finishes, and millwork.

### 5. Delivery
Inspection, final adjustments, and owner's manual.
